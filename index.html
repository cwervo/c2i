Time: Tuesdays, 4:00 PM ‐ 6:40 PM
Location: Parsons 2 W 13th, Room 1011
Instructor: Andrés Cuervo

Core 2: Interaction exposes students to thorough and elaborate interactive concepts and techniques for applications. It is an extensive investigation in the interface, the mechanism, the controls and the aims of interactive works. Students will learn how to design and develop complex interactive projects and understand how to undertake comprehensive research and direct their thinking process from brainstorming to the final outcome. They will be given the tools to conceive, plan and develop an interactive system and they will become aware of the importance of design’s role in contemporary media.
